PPA Ad on Right Rail for Flight Listing [Flighted]

To boost PPA Ad conversion on the flight listing page while keeping the mainline clean, we've shifted PPA ads from below the fold on the mainline to above the fold on the Right Rail. This strategic placement ensures that ads are more visible without cluttering the primary content, thereby enhancing user experience and potentially driving higher ad engagement.

Image Gallery on Destination Details [Shipped]

We’re excited to announce the launch of the Image Gallery on the Destination Details page on Bing Travel. This highly requested feature allows users to view stunning destination images in a dedicated gallery view. Additionally, relevant ads related to the destinations are shown, helping users with task completion. Destination BOP Answer on Bing [Shipped]

We're thrilled to announce the launch of our BOP answer feature for destination queries, achieving an impressive APSAT of ~50%. This innovative addition displays a curated mix of popular and recently viewed destinations at the bottom of the page. This enhances user experience by allowing users to seamlessly continue their search if initial results don’t meet their needs. This personalizes the experience, enabling quick returns to previously viewed destinations. Leveraging data on popular and recently viewed spots strategically places content for more effective and user-friendly search results.

Nearby Dates Carousel on L2 Flight Listing Page [Shipped]

Flights are often the most expensive part of travel planning. For leisure travelers on costly routes, flexible dates can help avoid peak times like festivals or weekends. The Nearby Dates Carousel on the L2 Flight Listing Page helps users find cheaper options by appearing after the fifth item. When shown, it boosts the Flights List to Summary conversion rate by ~12% on Desktop and 20% on mobile, and the Flights List to Booking conversion rate by ~20% on Desktop and 27% on mobile. Additionally, PCR and module CTR increase by 1%.

Flight Summary Redesign [Shipped]

The Flights Summary page is a key step in the booking process, where users transition to external providers to finalize their flight booking. Our main goal here is to build user confidence and encourage them to proceed. The new design features a two-column layout, allowing users to view booking providers and confirm itinerary details in one glance. Additionally, with PPA Ads and cross-sell modules, users can explore other travel options alongside their flight booking. During the flight of this new design, Travel Hub flights revenue has increased by ~28% on desktop, the overall List to booking rate is up by ~20%, the summary to booking rate by ~15% (desktop), and PCR has improved by ~1% on both desktop and mobile.

Health Expert Edit Profile [Shipped]

We’re excited to announce the launch of the new "Edit Profile" feature on the Health Experts Platform! Experts can now easily update and refine their profile details, ensuring that their information is accurate and up-to-date. In addition, we've introduced a profile preview option within the editing process, allowing experts to see how their profile will appear before submitting changes.
This update not only helps experts provide the correct information, increasing their chances of profile approval, but also significantly reduces the workload for our admin team. We're confident this enhancement will streamline the experience for everyone involved!

Bing Unified Health Hub [Flighted]

As part of the ongoing migration of health content from MSN to Bing, we've been evaluating the feasibility of rendering MSN health content on bing.com/health using an iframe. Our testing focuses on several technical aspects, including content compatibility, load performance, responsiveness across devices, and overall user experience.
We are also assessing potential security concerns, such as cross-origin resource sharing (CORS) restrictions, and ensuring the iframe implementation aligns with Bing’s design and SEO guidelines. These tests are crucial to ensuring a smooth and efficient transition while delivering high-quality, accessible health content on Bing.

PAA Magazine Answer cards [Shipped]

In Magazine Answer Card Framework, we have successfully integrated a new card from the People Also Ask section. This card is now prominently featured within the framework. In the Magazine Answer, we have enabled the PAA card to be displayed in various formats, including square, portrait, and landscape, ensuring a versatile and engaging user experience.

Making Whole card clickable in Mainline PAA [InFlight]

The entire card is now fully clickable, effectively resolving previous issues related to clickability. To enhance functionality and maintainability, a new component was developed specifically for the attribution section. This new component replaces the previous dependency on the partner component, thereby simplifying the overall maintenance of the card. Additionally, unused code was meticulously cleaned up, making view file management more straightforward. This improved experience is currently being implemented. This feature is expected to have HTML and APSAT gains in the flight.
